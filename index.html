Introduction

Foundation January 27, 1990

Sultan was founded by Arshad Shamim, who is now presently the Director of the very company which he set up on 27th January 1990 which sold Kurtas and Sherwani with not a varied collection. On 27th January 2000, Sultan became Sultans Apparels Pvt. Ltd. which sold Kurta, Sherwani, Ethnic accessories, Grooms wear, children's wear etc. This company provides a huge variety of Collection to satisfy customers from all over the country to across the border. Sultans Apparels are affiliated to Big bazar, Reliance.

About Founder

Arshad Shamim, the brain behind this huge company now started his business long back. He lost his father at 9 years age. Sultan started when the founder opened a small kurta stall which sold Kurtas at Rs. 99. The huge breakthrough came when his clothes made Rs. 1 Lakh wholesale.There was no turning point then and was officially founded on the auspicious occasion of Holy Ramzan. The company which is now the biggest wholesaler in the market started of with just 15 second hand machines. The innovative lookout and efficient management brought out the best results for the company.
